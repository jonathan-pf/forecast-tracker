**AGI before 2030 (18%):** Expert consensus clusters around ~25% by 2029/2030. Adjusted down for diminishing scaling returns, data exhaustion headwinds, and definitional ambiguity. Main upside risk: architectural breakthroughs or synthetic data proving viable at scale.

**Consultancy AI disruption (8%):** The industry is growing 4-6% annually and large firms are positioning as AI beneficiaries, not victims. A 33% decline in under 5 years would be historically unprecedented for professional services. Main upside risk: rapid client in-sourcing of AI analytics capability.

**Labour largest party (28%):** Labour polling at ~18%, a historic mid-term collapse. Reform UK at ~27% is the dominant force. However, 3.75 years is substantial recovery time, FPTP may suppress Reform's seat count, and tactical anti-Reform voting is emerging. Main upside risk: leadership change + economic recovery + tactical voting consolidation.
